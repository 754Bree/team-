WHO WE ARE

Transform Empowerment for Action Initiative (TEAM) is a grass-root civil society organization registered in 2009
targeting Women, Children, Youths and Persons living with dissabilities with a common aim of
promoting democratic governance and human rights in Nyanza and Western Kenya.

Vision

An Empowered and Transformed Society

Mission

To promote Democratic governance, constitutionalism and Human Rights.

Our Values

Integrity and Tolerance
Equity and Equality
Accountability and Transpiracy
