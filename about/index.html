ABOUT TEAM

Background

Transform Empowerment for Action Initiative (TEAM)began as a grass-root Civil Society Organization (CSO) and was registered in 2009.
TEAMS' target audience majorly consists of Women, Children, Youths and Persons living with Disabilities.
Its main aim is to promote Democratic Governance and Human Rights in Nyanza and Western Kenya. As a way of fulfilling its obligations
and promise to the citizenry by ensuring that they are well informed on all
their rights and Responsibilities as Kenyans, TEAM has partnered with other like minded Organizations in a bid to Create Awareness and Promote Social Accountability within the Health Sector for purposes of improving Health Service Delivery all over the county.

OUR PARTNERS

URAIA TRUST

URAIA is a charitable trust organization that deals with Civic Education, Civic Engagement and Institutional Transformation .
TEAM partnered with URAIA TRUST in three major projects that ensured that the citizens were given adequate Civic education as a way to enlighten them on their rights and obligations as duty-bearers.
