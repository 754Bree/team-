ABOUT TEAM

Background

Transform Empowerment for Action Initiative (TEAM) began as a grass-root Civil Society Organization (CSO) and was registered in 2009.
TEAMS' target audience majorly consists of Women, Children, Youths and Persons living with Disabilities.
Its main aim is to promote Democratic Governance and Human Rights in Nyanza and Western Kenya.
As a way of fulfilling its obligations and promise to the citizenry by ensuring that they are well
informed on all their rights and Responsibilities as Kenyans, TEAM
has partnered with other like minded Organizations in
a bid to Create Awareness and Promote
Social Accountability within the
Health Sectorfor purposes of
improving Health Service
Delivery all over
the county.

URAIA TRUST

URAIA is a charitable trust organization that deals with
Civic Education, Civic Engagement and Institutional Transformation.
TEAM partnered with URAIA TRUST in three major
projects that ensured that citizens were given adequate

FREDRICH ALBERT STIFTUNG (FES)

Fredrich Ebert Foundation is a German based political foundaton associated with the Social Democratic Party of Germany, yet independent of it.
