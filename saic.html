Background

TEAM with support from URAIA-Trust implemented a project dubbed The Safe and Inclusive Cities,
a project implemented in Kisumu county by three major partners
i.e PLAN International, SDI-K and URAIA Trust.
The foundation for the intervention was to support the development of strong,dynamic and diverse
civil society at all levels of the city as a platform for young women and men's participation
in urban governance and development processes.
The projects main outcome was increased citizen participation and engagement wiyh the Security Service.
